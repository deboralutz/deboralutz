Lichtara Research Initiative

Lichtara operates as a research platform within the broader structural program on governability and institutional design under hybrid agency.

It focuses specifically on living authorship regimes, ethical licensing architectures, and formal differentiation of agency in collaborative human–AI systems.

It explores institutional design under conditions of hybrid human–AI collaboration, focusing on transparency, accountability, and structured co-authorship without transfer of moral or juridical sovereignty.

The initiative integrates legal architecture, systems philosophy, and governance theory.
