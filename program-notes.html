Program Notes

This research program advances a structural theory of governability under conditions of distributed and hybrid human–AI agency.

The central thesis is that governability depends not on performance or optimization, but on the sustained interaction of three irreducible structural conditions: Field, Coherence, and Limit.

The long-term objective is the consolidation of a minimal structural grammar of political order applicable across institutional, legal, and technological domains.

Current Development Axes

  • Triadic Structural Theory of Governability
  • Structural Stability and Cascading Rupture
  • Institutional Stewardship under Hybrid Agency
  • Living Authorship and Structural Differentiation

These notes function as a living document of the program's conceptual orientation and ongoing development.
